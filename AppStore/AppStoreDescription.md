## Full Description

**Stop forgetting. Start packing.**

You've been there. Standing at the airport gate, phone in hand, trying to remember: did I pack the charger? Did I remember the toothbrush? Haul ends that anxiety — forever.

**How it works:**

📸 **Photograph your suitcase** — Before you pack a single thing, snap a photo of your empty bag. This is your canvas.

✅ **Add your packing list** — Browse our curated suggestions or type in exactly what you need. T-shirts, toiletries, passport, electronics — whatever your trip requires.

✏️ **Tap as you pack** — Each item in your list becomes a satisfying checkbox. Tap when it's packed. Watch the green checkmarks accumulate.

🧳 **Pre-trip smarts** — Haul reminds you to check your bag before departure. It nags you about your passport. It even knows the weather at your destination (on Travel plan).

**No account. No cloud. Just your phone.**

All your trips, all your photos, all your packing lists — stored locally on your device. No sign-up. No subscription required for the basics.

**Haul Free:**
- Track 2 trips
- 20 items per trip
- 1 suitcase photo per trip

**Haul Pack ($2.99/mo):**
- Unlimited trips
- Unlimited items
- Unlimited photo storage
- Category organization
- Pre-departure reminders

**Haul Travel ($5.99/mo):**
- Everything in Pack
- Multiple suitcases per trip
- Packing templates for any trip type
- Weather at your destination
- Trip statistics
- Priority support

**Built for:**
- Frequent travelers
- Weekend warriors
- Business trippers
- Adventure seekers
- Anyone who's ever wondered "did I pack X?"
